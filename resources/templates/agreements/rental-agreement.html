6. USE OF PREMISES: The PREMISES shall be used only for [PURPOSE] purposes and shall not be sub-let without the written consent of the LANDLORD.

7. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this agreement by giving [NOTICE_PERIOD] days prior written notice to the other party.

8. BREACH: In case of any breach of terms and conditions by the TENANT, the LANDLORD shall have the right to terminate this agreement and evict the TENANT.
