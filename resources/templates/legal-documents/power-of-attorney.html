GENERAL POWER OF ATTORNEY

KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS that I, [PRINCIPAL_NAME], son/daughter of [PRINCIPAL_FATHER], aged [PRINCIPAL_AGE] years, residing at [PRINCIPAL_ADDRESS] (hereinafter called the "PRINCIPAL") do hereby nominate, constitute and appoint [ATTORNEY_NAME], son/daughter of [ATTORNEY_FATHER], aged [ATTORNEY_AGE] years, residing at [ATTORNEY_ADDRESS] (hereinafter called the "ATTORNEY") to be my true and lawful Attorney for me and in my name and on my behalf to do and execute all or any of the following acts, deeds and things:

1. To manage, conduct and carry on my business of [BUSINESS_DETAILS] and to do all acts, deeds and things necessary for the said purpose.

2. To operate my bank accounts maintained with [BANK_NAMES] and to withdraw, deposit, transfer funds and issue cheques.

3. To sell, transfer, lease, mortgage or otherwise deal with my movable and immovable properties.

4. To appear before any Government Department, Court, Tribunal or Authority and represent me in all matters.

5. To sign, execute and deliver all documents, deeds, agreements and contracts on my behalf.

6. To collect all monies due to me from any person or institution.

7. To pay all my taxes, bills, dues and liabilities.

8. To do all other acts and things which I could have done if personally present.

AND I HEREBY DECLARE that all acts, deeds and things done by my said Attorney pursuant to this Power of Attorney shall be binding upon me as if done by me personally.

AND I HEREBY FURTHER DECLARE that this Power of Attorney shall remain in force from [START_DATE] to [END_DATE] unless revoked by me in writing.
